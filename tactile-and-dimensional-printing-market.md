<p><p>The tactile and dimensional printing market encompasses various techniques that enhance printing through texture and depth. Gravure printing offers high-quality, continuous patterns, ideal for large runs. Flexography utilizes flexible relief plates for versatile printing on diverse materials, making it cost-effective for packaging. Screen printing uses mesh screens to transfer ink, suitable for thick layers and vivid colors on various substrates. Inkjet printing, known for its precision, creates detailed images and textures, allowing for custom designs and short runs.</p></p>
