<p><p>The band pass optical filter market is divided into single-band and multiband types. Single-band filters transmit a specific wavelength range while blocking others, making them ideal for applications requiring precise wavelength selection, such as spectroscopy and telecommunications. Multiband filters, on the other hand, allow multiple wavelength ranges to pass through simultaneously. These are useful in applications like imaging and sensing, where various spectral bands are needed. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including healthcare, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
