<p><ul><li>Bullet Mufflers</li><li>Chambered Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car muffler market features various types, including bullet and chambered mufflers. Bullet mufflers are compact, cylindrical designs that provide a straightforward, aggressive sound, favored in performance vehicles for their minimal restriction. In contrast, chambered mufflers utilize internal chambers to control sound waves, producing a deep, resonant tone while promoting efficient exhaust flow. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences, with bullet mufflers appealing to those seeking a sporty edge, and chambered mufflers attracting users desiring a balanced sound and performance.</p></p>
