<p><p>The surgical adhesives and sealants market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Fibrin sealants mimic natural clotting to promote wound healing. Collagen-based adhesives enhance tissue adhesion and regeneration, while gelatin-based adhesives provide temporary bonding for surgical applications. Cyanoacrylates offer rapid bonding but are limited to specific uses. Polymeric hydrogels provide flexibility and moisture support for wounds. Urethane-based adhesives combine strength and flexibility, suitable for a range of surgical procedures, ensuring effective tissue approximation and sealing.</p></p>
