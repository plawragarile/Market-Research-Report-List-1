<p><strong>The FPD Etcher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Etcher</li><li>Wet Etcher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FPD (Flat Panel Display) etching technologies are categorized into two main markets: dry etchers and wet etchers. Dry etching utilizes plasma or gases to remove material from the substrate, offering precise control and reduced chemical waste, making it ideal for intricate patterns. In contrast, wet etching employs liquid solutions to dissolve materials, allowing for larger area processing but less precision. Both methods are crucial in FPD manufacturing for creating high-quality displays with specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
