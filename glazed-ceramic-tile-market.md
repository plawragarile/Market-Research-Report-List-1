<p><p>The glazed ceramic tile market is categorized into three main types based on finish: matte, semi-gloss, and high-gloss. Matte tiles offer a non-reflective surface, providing a subtle, elegant look while reducing glare. Semi-gloss tiles strike a balance between sheen and practicality, reflecting some light while being easier to clean than matte options. High-gloss tiles feature a shiny finish that enhances colors and brightness, making spaces appear larger but requiring more maintenance to avoid visible smudges and scratches. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional needs in design projects.</p></p>
