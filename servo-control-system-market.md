<p><p>The servo control system market encompasses two primary types: analog and digital servo systems. Analog servo systems utilize continuous voltage signals to control motion, offering simplicity and reliability for basic applications. In contrast, digital servo systems employ microprocessors and discrete signals for enhanced precision, flexibility, and advanced functionalities. Digital systems enable complex algorithms and real-time feedback, making them ideal for high-performance applications in robotics, aerospace, and automation. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, balancing cost, complexity, and performance.</p></p>
