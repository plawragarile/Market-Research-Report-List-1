<p><p>A bone conduction headset is a type of audio device that transmits sound vibrations through the bones of the skull directly to the inner ear, bypassing the eardrums. This technology allows users to hear ambient sounds while still enjoying audio through the headset, making it popular for activities like running, cycling, and other outdoor sports.</p><p>The market for bone conduction headsets is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer demand for hands-free communication and the rising adoption of these devices in various sectors such as healthcare, military, and entertainment. The growing awareness of hearing health and the advantages of bone conduction technology are also contributing to market expansion.
