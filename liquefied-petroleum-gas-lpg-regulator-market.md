<p><ul><li>Direct Acting Type</li><li>Spring-Loaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) regulator market features two primary types: direct acting and spring-loaded regulators. Direct acting regulators operate by allowing gas flow based on diaphragm movement directly influenced by pressure changes, providing a simple and responsive pressure regulation. In contrast, spring-loaded regulators utilize a spring mechanism to control gas flow, offering more stability and efficiency under varying pressure conditions. Both types cater to different applications in residential and commercial settings, ensuring safe and efficient LPG usage.</p></p>
