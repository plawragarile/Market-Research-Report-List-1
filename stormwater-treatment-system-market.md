<p><strong>The Stormwater Treatment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screening System</li><li>Separation System</li><li>Filtration System</li><li>Infiltration System</li><li>Retention System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stormwater treatment system market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Screening System**: Removes large debris from stormwater using screens. </p><p>2. **Separation System**: Uses density differences to separate pollutants from water. </p><p>3. **Filtration System**: Cleans water by passing it through filter media to capture impurities. </p><p>4. **Infiltration System**: Allows water to percolate into the ground, promoting groundwater recharge. </p><p>5. **Retention System**: Stores water in ponds or basins to allow sedimentation and evaporation. </p><p>6. **Other**: Encompasses emerging and specialized technologies for stormwater management.</p></p>
