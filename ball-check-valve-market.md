<p><p>A ball check valve is a type of non-return valve that uses a spherical ball to prevent backflow in a piping system. This design allows fluid to flow in one direction while automatically sealing off the reverse flow when the fluid pressure drops or flows in the opposite direction. Ball check valves are commonly used in various industries, including water and wastewater management, oil and gas, and chemical processing, due to their reliability and efficiency in maintaining system integrity.</p><p>The Ball Check Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for efficient fluid control systems across various sectors, particularly in industrial applications.
