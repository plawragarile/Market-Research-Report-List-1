<p><p>The linear valves market encompasses various types, including gate valves, diaphragm valves, and others. Gate valves are primarily used to start or stop flow, providing minimal resistance when fully opened. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm to control flow, offering excellent sealing and are ideal for handling corrosive fluids. Other linear valves can include globe valves and pinch valves, each designed for specific applications in industries like water treatment, oil and gas, and pharmaceuticals, emphasizing efficient flow regulation.</p></p>
