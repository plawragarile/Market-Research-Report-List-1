<p><p>The water filters market encompasses various technologies to ensure clean drinking water. Reverse osmosis (RO) water filters utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants and dissolved solids, providing highly purified water. Ultrafiltration membrane water filters operate at a higher pore size, effectively removing suspended solids and bacteria while retaining essential minerals. Other market types may include activated carbon filters, ceramic filters, and UV disinfection systems, each catering to different filtration needs and water quality issues.</p></p>
