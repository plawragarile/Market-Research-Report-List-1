<p><ul><li>Open Loop System</li><li>Closed Loop System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Web Tension Control market is classified into two main types: Open Loop Systems and Closed Loop Systems. Open Loop Systems operate on preset parameters without real-time feedback, making them simpler and often less costly, but less precise. In contrast, Closed Loop Systems utilize sensors to continuously monitor and adjust the tension based on feedback, ensuring higher accuracy and consistency during web processing. The choice between the two often depends on application requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
