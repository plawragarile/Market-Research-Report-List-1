<p><ul><li>Static Verification</li><li>Dynamic Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Identity Verification Software can be categorized into two main types: Static and Dynamic Verification. Static Verification involves the assessment of unchanging documents, such as government IDs or passports, to validate an individual's identity at a single point in time. In contrast, Dynamic Verification adapts to real-time scenarios, utilizing biometric data like facial recognition or behavioral analysis for ongoing identity assurance. This distinction reflects the evolving nature of identity verification, with Dynamic solutions offering enhanced security and adaptability to fraud risks.</p></p>
