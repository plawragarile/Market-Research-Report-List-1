<p><p>The fan and blower motors market is classified into several types, primarily including centrifugal motors and axial flow motors. Centrifugal motors are designed to move air or gases radially, creating high pressure and flow, making them suitable for applications like HVAC systems. Axial flow motors move air along the axis, providing lower pressure with higher flow rates, ideal for ventilation. Other types may include specialty motors for unique applications, enhancing versatility in industrial, commercial, and residential uses.</p></p>
