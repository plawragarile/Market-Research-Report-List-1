<p><p>The watercolor market is categorized into three main types: transparent, opaque, and others. Opaque watercolors, on the other hand, have a higher pigment concentration, allowing them to cover surfaces more completely and produce vibrant colors. The "others" category encompasses specialty paints like iridescent or metallic watercolors, which offer unique textures and finishes, appealing to diverse artistic needs and preferences.</p></p>
