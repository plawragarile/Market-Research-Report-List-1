<p><ul><li>Stick Type</li><li>Dial Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tyre gauge market features three main types: stick type, dial type, and digital type. Stick type gauges are simple and portable, using a manual sliding scale to indicate pressure. Dial type gauges offer a more visual display with a needle on a dial, providing a good balance between accuracy and ease of use. Digital type gauges use electronic sensors to deliver precise readings on an LCD screen, often with additional features like automatic shut-off, making them user-friendly and efficient for consumers.</p></p>
