<p><p>The manual sandblasting machine market is divided into two primary types: dry and wet sandblasting. Dry sandblasting utilizes compressed air to propel abrasive materials on surfaces, making it ideal for quick and efficient cleaning or surface preparation. In contrast, wet sandblasting combines water with the abrasive, reducing dust and providing a smoother finish, making it suitable for delicate materials or environments where dust control is essential. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries, enhancing surface treatments effectively.</p></p>
