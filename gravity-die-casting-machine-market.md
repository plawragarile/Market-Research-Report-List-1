<p><p>The Gravity Die Casting Machine market features two primary types: Die Horizontal Opening Machines and Die Vertical Opening Machines. Horizontal opening machines facilitate the casting process by allowing molds to be opened sideways, making them suitable for larger components and enabling efficient metal flow. In contrast, vertical opening machines operate by lifting the upper part of the mold upwards, ideal for smaller parts and intricate designs, providing precision and improved control during casting. Both types cater to diverse industry needs in metal production.</p></p>
