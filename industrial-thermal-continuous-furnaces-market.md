<p><p>Industrial thermal continuous furnaces encompass various types, each designed for specific processing needs. Belt furnaces utilize a moving belt to transport materials through controlled temperature zones, ideal for uniform heating. Rotating hearth furnaces feature a circular platform that rotates, allowing for consistent exposure to heat. Pusher furnaces employ a mechanism to push materials through the furnace, ensuring efficient processing for larger batches. Other types may include shuttle furnaces or muffle furnaces, catering to specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
