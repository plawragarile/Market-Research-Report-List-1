<p><p>The front-loading sterilizer market encompasses various types based on sterilization methods. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam for efficient microbial elimination. Hot air sterilizers utilize dry heat for effective temperature-based sterilization. UV sterilizers employ ultraviolet light to destroy pathogens, while ethylene oxide sterilizers use gas for temperature-sensitive materials. Infrared sterilizers harness infrared radiation for surface disinfection, and ozone sterilizers utilize ozone gas for its potent antimicrobial properties. Other methods may include advanced technologies or hybrid systems.</p></p>
