<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Type</li><li>Transverse Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The finned tube heat exchangers market is primarily divided into two types: longitudinal and transverse. Longitudinal type heat exchangers have fins aligned parallel to the flow direction, optimizing heat transfer for fluids moving in the same direction. In contrast, transverse type heat exchangers feature fins arranged perpendicular to the flow, enhancing heat exchange efficiency in applications where cross-flow is predominant. Both types are widely used in various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and chemical processing, catering to distinct thermal management needs.</p></p>
