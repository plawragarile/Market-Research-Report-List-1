<p><p>The thermal ablation devices market includes several types that utilize heat to destroy tissue. Interstitial probes deliver energy directly to targeted areas, while grounded probes use an external grounding mechanism. Needle applications facilitate targeted delivery of thermal energy. Radiofrequency ablation employs radio waves to heat and eliminate tissue, whereas hydrothermal ablation uses heated fluid, and microwave ablation utilizes microwave energy to achieve similar results. Each type offers unique advantages for various medical applications, including cancer treatment and tissue management.</p></p>
