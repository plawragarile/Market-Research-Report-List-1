<p><strong>The Power Transmission Lines & Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Tension (HT)</li><li>Extra High Tension (EHT)</li><li>Ultra High Tension (UHT))</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power transmission lines and towers are categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: High Tension (HT), Extra High Tension (EHT), and Ultra High Tension (UHT). HT lines operate at voltages between 35 kV and 220 kV, facilitating regional energy distribution. EHT lines, ranging from 220 kV to 800 kV, are used for long-distance transmission, enhancing efficiency. UHT lines exceed 800 kV, capable of transmitting vast amounts of electricity over extensive distances, reducing energy loss and improving grid stability.</p></p>
