HALE UAVs are designed for long-duration missions at high altitudes, focusing on surveillance and reconnaissance. MALE UAVs operate at medium altitudes, offering versatility for various applications, including tactical operations. SUAVs are smaller, typically used for short-range missions, providing agility and ease of deployment for both military and civilian purposes.</p></p>
