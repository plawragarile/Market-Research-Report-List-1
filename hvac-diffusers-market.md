<p><p>The HVAC diffusers market encompasses three main types based on their mounting: ceiling-mounted, wall-mounted, and floor-mounted. Ceiling-mounted diffusers are commonly used for even air distribution in larger spaces, effectively promoting airflow from above. Wall-mounted diffusers are typically utilized in commercial environments, providing localized airflow at a suitable height for occupants. Floor-mounted diffusers, designed for installation at ground level, optimize heating and cooling by allowing air to flow directly from the floor, enhancing comfort and efficiency.</p></p>
