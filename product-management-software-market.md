<p><p>Product management software can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control and customizability but requiring significant maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on the provider’s servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and automatic updates. This model often follows a subscription pricing structure, making it more cost-effective for businesses seeking flexibility and collaboration tools across remote teams.</p></p>
