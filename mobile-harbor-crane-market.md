<p><ul><li>Gantry Crane</li><li>Half Gantry Crane</li><li>Double Cantilever Gantry Crane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile harbor crane market includes three main types: gantry cranes, half gantry cranes, and double cantilever gantry cranes. Gantry cranes are fully supported on two legs running on rails, ideal for heavy lifting in ports. Half gantry cranes have one support leg, allowing flexibility in space-constrained areas. Double cantilever gantry cranes feature two cantilevered arms on either side, providing enhanced reach and stability, making them suitable for larger marine operations. Each type serves specific operational needs in cargo handling.</p></p>
