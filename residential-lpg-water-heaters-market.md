<p><p>The residential LPG water heaters market is primarily divided into two types: tankless LPG water heaters and tank LPG water heaters. </p><p>Tankless LPG water heaters heat water on demand, providing an endless supply without storing it, making them energy-efficient and ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, tank LPG water heaters store hot water in a tank, offering a readily available supply but consuming more energy to maintain temperature. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for efficiency, space, and convenience in residential heating solutions.</p></p>
