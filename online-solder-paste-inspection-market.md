<p><ul><li>2D SPI</li><li>3D SPI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Online Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) market primarily consists of two key types: 2D SPI and 3D SPI. 2D SPI utilizes camera systems to capture two-dimensional images of solder paste deposits, ensuring correct volume and positioning. In contrast, 3D SPI employs advanced techniques like laser scanning to create three-dimensional profiles of the solder paste, allowing for more accurate measurements of height, volume, and shape. Both types are essential for maintaining quality control in electronic manufacturing processes.</p></p>
