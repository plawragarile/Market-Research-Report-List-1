<p><p>The photon counters market can be categorized into three main types. Basic type photon counters offer straightforward detection capabilities, focusing primarily on counting individual photons. Background compensation type counters are designed to filter out ambient noise, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy in detecting weak signals. Radiation source compensation type photon counters specifically adjust for interference from external radiation sources, ensuring reliable measurements even in complex environments. Each type caters to distinct applications in scientific research, medical imaging, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
