<p><p>The Basalt Fabric Yarns market is categorized into two key types: Basalt Chopped Fiber and Basalt Continuous Fiber. Basalt Chopped Fiber is produced by cutting basalt fibers into shorter lengths, making it suitable for composite materials and applications requiring bulk reinforcement. In contrast, Basalt Continuous Fiber consists of long, uninterrupted fibers, offering enhanced strength and flexibility for use in high-performance applications, such as aerospace, automotive, and construction. Both types leverage basalt's excellent thermal and mechanical properties in various industries.</p></p>
