<p><strong>The Berry Harvester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half-row Harvester</li><li>Full-row Harvester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The berry harvester market is primarily divided into two types: half-row harvesters and full-row harvesters. Half-row harvesters are designed to pick berries from one side of the row, allowing for greater maneuverability in narrower fields or rows. In contrast, full-row harvesters can harvest berries from both sides simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity in wider row configurations. Each type caters to different farming needs based on crop density, field layout, and operational scale, optimizing berry collection processes.</p></p>
