<p><p>The ceiling and in-wall speakers market can be categorized into active and passive speakers. Active speakers incorporate built-in amplification, making them easier to install and connect directly to audio sources. They typically offer enhanced sound quality and complex features like Bluetooth connectivity. Conversely, passive speakers require an external amplifier to function, allowing for greater customization of audio systems but demanding more installation effort. Each type caters to different user preferences, focusing on convenience or flexibility in sound management.</p></p>
