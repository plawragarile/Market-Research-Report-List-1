<p><p>Operational analytics software can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing more control over data security and customization. This option is often favored by organizations with strict compliance requirements or existing IT infrastructure investments.</p></p>
