<p><p>Stainless steel turbine meters are precision devices used for measuring fluid flow, and they come in two main types based on connection types: threaded and flanged. Threaded connection meters feature fittings that screw onto pipe ends, offering ease of installation in limited spaces. Flanged connection meters utilize raised or flat flanges that secure to pipelines, providing robust connections suitable for high-pressure environments. Both types are favored for their durability, accuracy, and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for various industrial applications.</p></p>
