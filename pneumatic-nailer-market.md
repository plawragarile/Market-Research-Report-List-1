<p><p>The pneumatic nailer market is primarily divided into two types: coil-style and strip-style pneumatic nailers. Coil-style nailers utilize a coiled strip of nails, allowing for continuous nailing and higher nail capacity, making them ideal for larger projects. In contrast, strip-style nailers use nails arranged in a straight strip, offering precision and ease of use for smaller tasks. Both types are popular in construction and woodworking, providing efficiency and speed in fastening applications.</p></p>
