<p><p>The flame-resistant hydraulic fluid market comprises four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing emulsions, while HFB fluids are water-free, non-emulsifiable fluids. HFC fluids are water-containing solutions that offer enhanced fire resistance, and HFD fluids are high-temperature fluids suitable for extreme conditions. Each type is designed to minimize fire risks in hydraulic systems, catering to diverse industrial needs, safety standards, and environmental considerations, thereby enhancing operational efficiency and safety in various sectors.</p></p>
