<p><p>The plastic antiblock additives market is primarily divided into two types: organic and inorganic antiblock additives. Organic antiblock additives, such as natural waxes and polymers, are derived from carbon-based materials and enhance the slip and anti-blocking properties of plastic films. Inorganic antiblock additives, including silica, calcium carbonate, and talc, are mineral-based and provide superior performance in preventing adhesion between layers of films. Both types cater to diverse applications in packaging, construction, and consumer goods, contributing to improved product functionality.</p></p>
