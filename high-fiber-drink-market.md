<p><p>The high fiber drink market is categorized into soluble and insoluble fiber drinks. Soluble fiber drinks contain fibers that dissolve in water, aiding in digestion and helping to regulate blood sugar levels, often providing a creamy texture. In contrast, insoluble fiber drinks consist of fibers that do not dissolve, promoting regular bowel movements and digestive health. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking to enhance their dietary fiber intake, support gut health, and manage weight effectively.</p></p>
