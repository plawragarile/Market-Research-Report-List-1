<p><strong>The GPS Module with Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active GPS Attenna Module</li><li>Passive GPS Attenna Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS module market is primarily divided into active and passive GPS antenna modules. Active GPS antenna modules incorporate built-in amplifiers to boost signal reception, making them suitable for environments with weak signals. They provide enhanced performance and are commonly used in automotive and mobile applications. In contrast, passive GPS antenna modules do not amplify the signal and rely on external devices for power, making them simpler and more affordable. They are often used in cost-sensitive applications where size and weight are critical.</p></p>
