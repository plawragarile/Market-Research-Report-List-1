<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Wind-driven Generator</li><li>Synchronous Wind-driven Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind-driven generator market is primarily divided into two types: asynchronous and synchronous generators. Asynchronous wind-driven generators, also known as induction generators, operate without needing synchronous speed, making them suitable for variable wind conditions. They are widely used due to their robustness and simplicity. Conversely, synchronous wind-driven generators maintain constant speed and require precise control, offering better efficiency and power quality. Both types play crucial roles in the growing renewable energy sector, catering to different operational needs and applications.</p></p>
