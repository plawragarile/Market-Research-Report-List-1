<p><p>The synthetic fatliquors market is categorized into three main types: anionic, cationic, and non-ionic. Anionic fatliquors are negatively charged, suitable for use in leather processing to improve softness and flexibility. Cationic fatliquors, being positively charged, enhance water repellency and dye uptake, making them ideal for specific leather qualities. Non-ionic fatliquors, lacking any charge, provide versatility and compatibility with various materials, promoting durability and softness without altering the leather's dye characteristics. Each type serves distinct applications in the leather industry.</p></p>
