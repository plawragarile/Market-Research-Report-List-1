<p><strong>The Perovskite Solar Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Structure</li><li>Inverted Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Perovskite Solar Cell market is dominated by two main structures: normal and inverted. Normal structure cells feature a configuration where the perovskite layer is sandwiched between a charge transport layer and a conductive electrode, optimizing light absorption and charge separation. In contrast, inverted structure cells have the perovskite layer positioned between the hole transport layer and the electrode, enhancing stability and compatibility with flexible substrates. Both structures aim to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and advance commercialization in solar technology.</p></p>
