<p><p>The bathroom exhaust fan market features three primary types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are designed for overhead installation, efficiently removing humidity and odors while blending seamlessly into the room's design. Wall-mounted fans are installed on walls, offering targeted ventilation and easy access for maintenance. Window-mounted fans fit directly into the window frame, providing a portable solution for effective airflow and ventilation. Each type serves unique space requirements and user preferences in maintaining a comfortable bathroom environment.</p></p>
