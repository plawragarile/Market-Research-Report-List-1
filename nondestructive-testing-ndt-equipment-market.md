<p><p>The Nondestructive Testing (NDT) equipment market encompasses various techniques essential for assessing materials without causing damage. Laser testing utilizes high-precision lasers to detect surface flaws, offering quick and accurate results. Magnetic flux leakage identifies defects in ferromagnetic materials by measuring changes in magnetic fields. Magnetic particle testing reveals surface and near-surface discontinuities through magnetic particles applied to the material. Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves to penetrate materials, identifying internal flaws and offering comprehensive evaluations across industries.</p></p>
